About-Game

A flip the card game is a memory-based game where players attempt to find matching pairs of cards by flipping them over two at a time. Players take turns flipping over two cards, trying to remember their positions to make matches. The game typically continues until all the pairs are found, and the player with the most matches wins. It's often played as a recreational or educational activity, helping to enhance memory and cognitive skills.
